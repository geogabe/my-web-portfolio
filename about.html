I'm a senior product designer and creative director with over 19 years of experience shaping digital products — from zero-to-one startups to enterprise platforms serving thousands of users worldwide.

Background

Over nearly two decades I've built products across 14 industries — from fintech and trading platforms to IT infrastructure, healthcare, and logistics. I've served as founding designer on 8 products, which means I'm used to building design systems, teams, and culture from scratch.

My approach blends rigorous UX thinking with a strong visual sensibility and a genuine fluency in data — I'm equally comfortable whiteboarding with engineers, presenting to boards, and getting hands-on in Figma.

I've spent the last several years leading design at Nexthink, a B2B SaaS company building the future of Digital Employee Experience, where I shaped the Infinity platform, Amplify (a browser extension used by millions of IT agents), and a suite of AI-driven IT tools.
