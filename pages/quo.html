Challenge

Private wealth advisors were managing complex, multi-asset portfolios across disconnected tools — no unified interface for tracking positions, executing trades, and advising high-net-worth clients simultaneously. The challenge was to build a platform dense enough for professional traders but refined enough to use in front of clients.

Process

As founding designer, I built the design language from scratch — starting with the core data model and working outward to navigation, density patterns, and interaction flows. Extensive research into Bloomberg, Refinitiv, and bespoke wealth tools informed what conventions to keep and what to challenge.

Outcome

A sophisticated cross-platform wealth management platform — covering portfolio overview, security research, order execution, and client reporting in one coherent experience. Clean enough for advisor-client meetings, powerful enough for active trading sessions.
