Challenge

Private wealth advisors were managing complex, multi-asset portfolios across disconnected tools — no unified interface for tracking positions, executing trades, and advising high-net-worth clients simultaneously. The challenge was to build a platform dense enough for professional traders but refined enough to use in front of clients.

Process

With a PM and another product designer, we built the design language from scratch — starting with the core data model and working outward to navigation, density patterns, and interaction flows. Extensive research into Bloomberg, Refinitiv, and bespoke wealth tools informed what conventions to keep and what to challenge.

Outcome

Fully Al driven through NLP, Quo is the next generation wealth management platform the selected few have been asking for and the many now know they need. Intuitive and tailored, smart by design and fundamentally built for the modern world.

The platform
