Challenge

Enterprise data teams lacked a way to understand, govern, and activate the metadata sitting across their systems. Valuable intelligence — data lineage, ownership, quality scores, relationships — was buried in disconnected stores, making it invisible to the people who needed it most.

Process

I designed an ecosystem approach, treating the metadata graph as the core model and building modular views for discovery, lineage, and governance on top of it. The design was validated with data engineers and data stewards across multiple enterprise clients to ensure it matched real operational workflows.

Outcome

A metadata analytics ecosystem that gives data teams a unified lens on their entire data landscape — from discovery and lineage tracing to quality monitoring and governance workflows. The full case study includes detailed walkthroughs of each module.

The ecosystem

DataDNA's interface surfaces the hidden intelligence of enterprise metadata — connecting datasets, showing lineage paths, surfacing quality issues, and giving data stewards the tools to govern at scale. The full case study PDF walks through the complete platform in detail.
