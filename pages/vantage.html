Challenge

Teradata's Vantage platform was technically among the most powerful analytics engines in the world — but its UX hadn't kept pace. Complex analytics workflows remained inaccessible to analysts without deep SQL expertise, limiting the platform's reach and leaving business value on the table for enterprise clients.

Process

I led the design team through discovery of analyst workflows — from raw data ingestion through to business insight delivery — across multiple enterprise sectors. The design centred on a modular experience that scaled gracefully from first-time users to power analysts, without compromising depth for either.

Outcome

A redesigned UX that opened Vantage to a significantly broader enterprise user base — contributing to key strategic client wins and helping unlock over $127M in new opportunities. The design was presented at Teradata Universe conference as the platform's future experience direction.
