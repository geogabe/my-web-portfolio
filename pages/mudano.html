Challenge

Sharktower's delivery management platform had grown organically and needed a structural redesign — but with hundreds of active users at major financial services firms relying on it daily, a disruptive overhaul wasn't an option. The challenge was to deliver a meaningfully improved experience through a phased approach that preserved existing workflows.

Process

I began with a thorough audit of existing workflows and in-depth interviews with active users to understand which patterns were genuinely embedded versus which were workarounds they'd built around friction. This informed a phased redesign plan: fix the structural issues first, then evolve the visual language incrementally.

Outcome

Phase 1 delivered a cleaner, more structured interface that reduced cognitive load across the platform's core delivery tracking and reporting flows. The redesign was adopted without friction by existing users — laying a stable foundation for the next generation of Sharktower's product roadmap.
